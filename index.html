Welcome to Our Restaurant!

We are so glad you found us. For over 15 years, we have been serving up delicious homecooked meals made with care and quality ingredients sourced from local farms and producers. Step into our comfortable downtown eatery and you'll immediately feel the warmth and hospitality. The aroma of freshly baked pies and soups bubbling on the stove will have you ready to dig in.

Welcome to Our Restaurant!

Feel free to linger over a leisurely meal or grab something quick to go - either way, you'll leave satisfied. For breakfast, our fluffy omelets and pile of pancakes are legendary. Lunches feature soups, sandwiches and salads with seasonal flair. As evening falls, nothing beats a hot meal to unwind from the day. Old-fashioned favorites like pot roast, fried chicken and meatloaf are customer favorites. Don't forget to save room for a slice of pie!
